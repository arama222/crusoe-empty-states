Choose your disk type
Persistent disk
1 GiB – 10 TiB · $0.08/GiB/mo
Single VM · Block storage
Attach to one VM in read-write mode, or multiple VMs read-only. Up to 16 per instance. Attach and detach while running or stopped — unmount before detaching.
Shared disk
1 TiB – 1000 TiB · $0.07/GiB/mo
Multi-VM · NFS
Concurrent NFS access from many VMs at once. Up to 8 per instance, in 1 TiB increments. Max 36-character name. Data preserved until explicitly deleted.
